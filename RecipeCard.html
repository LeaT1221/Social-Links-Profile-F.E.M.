Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Preparation: 5 minutes.
  • Cook Time: 5 minutes.
  • Total: 10 minutes.

Ingredients







Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with your desired amount of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. For a fluffier texture, add in a tablespoon of milk or water.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heating, adding the butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter or oil is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure that the eggs coat the entire surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle in your chosen fillings over one have of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one side and fold it over the fillings, encapsulating them in the egg. Let it cook for a minute longer, then slide your omelette onto a plate and serve.
  6. Enjoy! Serve fresh from the pan, with the optional addition of extra salt and pepper, fresh herbs, or hot sauce/salsa.
